What Your Coating Does

  • Blocks UV & oxidation — shields your clear coat from sun fade and chalking.Very effective
  • Resists chemicals & stains — bird droppings, sap, bug guts and water spots sit on top and rinse off before they etch.Strong
  • Sheds water & holds gloss — water sheets off, dirt won't bond, washes get quicker and the deep shine lasts for years.Excellent

What It Does NOT Do

A ceramic coating and paint correction are not the same as PPF (paint protection film). The coating is a thin, hard chemical and UV barrier built for gloss and easy cleaning.

It will not stop rock chips, road-debris gouges, or door dings, and it won't fill or prevent deep scratches. For physical impact protection, that's PPF — a different product. We'll always be straight with you about which one fits your goals.

We don't rush coatings. A ceramic is only as good as the prep beneath it, so every job gets meticulous correction and slow, careful, panel-by-panel application — that care is what you're paying for, and what makes the finish last.

Keep Your Guarantee Valid

  • Wait at least 7 days before the first wash — let the coating fully cure.
  • Wash by hand at home; skip automatic tunnels and brush car washes.
  • Use a pH-neutral car soap — avoid harsh chemicals and degreasers.
  • Don't let bird droppings, sap, or bug guts sit — rinse them off promptly.
  • One annual check-up & top-up with us — from $90/yr — keeps the coating (and guarantee) alive.

Space, Cure & Logistics

  • Ceramic work needs a spacious garage — clean, covered, and dust-free for the cure.
  • No garage? We offer free vehicle pickup within ~20 miles of our Harrisburg & Charlotte locations — we'll confirm with you directly.
  • If we pick it up, the car stays with us through the overnight cure.
  • Plan for the vehicle to be out of service about a full day for coating jobs.
  • Coating cures overnight; no washing for the first 7 days after pickup.
